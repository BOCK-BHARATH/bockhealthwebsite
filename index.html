Health Innovation Roadmap

A clear sequence of milestones guiding our journey to automated healthcare.

  1. Phase 0 — Training/Internship

    Healthcare technology fundamentals, team setup and medical AI foundation.

  2. Phase 1

    Building a prescribing robot (including the AI LLM integration with Nvidia Jetson).

  3. Phase 2

    R and D for building surgical robots.Building the MVP (If MSME/Nidhi Prayas/any other grants are approved).

  4. Phase 3

    Building surgical robots virtually with CAD, electronics, and simulation testing.

  5. Phase 4

    Building an Automated Hospitals virtually (including the LLM, testing and validation in simulation/softwares).

  6. Phase 5

    Going out for funding. Estimated to be 50Cr.

  7. Phase 6

    Building an Automated Hospitals.

  8. Phase 7

    The launch of our Automated Hospital to the public.
